Download JEE Mains Past Year Question Papers with Answer Key and Solution in PDF

JEE Main examination is conducted in online mode by NTA in two shifts on multiple days. The difficulty level of JEE Main paper is the same for all days and shifts. Here you can download JEE Main past year papers with answer key and solution in PDF format.

Every JEE aspirant must solve previous year papers of JEE Main for better preparation of the exam. It's recommended to solve JEE Main full paper tests after you've gone through the complete syllabus and have a clear understanding of every topic in all the chapters. Practice JEE Mains previous years papers and online mock tests in a time bound manner, like you are solving the real paper in a 3 hour duration.
